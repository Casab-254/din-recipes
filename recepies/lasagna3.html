So, without further ado, let's get cookin'.

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by browning ground beef and mixing with marinara sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
